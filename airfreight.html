Air Freight FAQs

Answers to common questions about our air freight services

Air freight is significantly faster than ocean shipping. Typical transit times:

  • Middle East to Europe: 1-2 days
  • Asia to Middle East: 2-3 days
  • USA to Middle East: 3-5 days
  • Within Middle East: Same day or next day

These times are door-to-door and include customs clearance where applicable.

Standard limitations for passenger aircraft:

  • Maximum weight per piece: Typically 100-150 kg
  • Maximum dimensions: 120 x 80 x 80 cm (varies by airline)
  • Oversized cargo requires special handling and freighters

For cargo aircraft, limitations are much higher. Contact us for specific oversized requirements.

The main documents typically required include:

  • Air Waybill (AWB)
  • Commercial Invoice
  • Packing List
  • Certificate of Origin
  • Import/Export Licenses (if applicable)
  • Dangerous Goods Declaration (if applicable)

Our team will prepare all necessary documentation based on your specific shipment.

Yes, we specialize in temperature-controlled air freight for:

  • Pharmaceuticals and vaccines
  • Perishable foods and flowers
  • Chemical and biological samples
  • Other temperature-sensitive products

We provide active and passive cooling solutions with real-time temperature monitoring throughout transit.

For high-value shipments, we implement multiple security measures:

  • Dedicated secure handling procedures
  • Tamper-evident seals and packaging
  • GPS tracking for door-to-door visibility
  • Armed escorts available upon request
  • Specialized insurance coverage

All high-value shipments receive personal supervision from our security-trained staff.
