Our Ocean Freight Solutions

FCL (Full Container Load)

Ideal for large shipments that require exclusive use of a container. We offer competitive rates and guaranteed space on major shipping lines.

  • 20', 40', and 40' High Cube container options
  • Door-to-door, port-to-port, or multimodal solutions
  • Weekly departures to all major global ports
  • Real-time container tracking

LCL (Less than Container Load)

Cost-effective solution for smaller shipments that don't require a full container. We consolidate cargo with other shipments heading to the same destination.

  • Weekly consolidated sailings to global destinations
  • Cargo insurance options available
  • Expert cargo packing and crating services
  • Door-to-door delivery options

Ocean Freight FAQs

Answers to common questions about our ocean freight services

FCL (Full Container Load) means your goods occupy an entire container exclusively, while LCL (Less than Container Load) means your shipment shares container space with other cargo. FCL is typically more cost-effective for larger shipments (usually more than 10-15 cubic meters), while LCL is better for smaller shipments.

Transit times vary depending on the origin, destination, and routing. For example:

  • China to Middle East: 14-21 days
  • Europe to Middle East: 10-18 days
  • USA to Middle East: 25-35 days

These are sailing times only and don't include inland transportation or customs clearance.

The main documents typically required include:

  • Commercial Invoice
  • Packing List
  • Bill of Lading (B/L)
  • Certificate of Origin
  • Import/Export Licenses (if applicable)
  • Dangerous Goods Declaration (if applicable)

Our team will guide you through all documentation requirements specific to your shipment.

Yes, we offer comprehensive customs clearance services in all countries we serve. Our team of licensed customs brokers ensures your shipments clear customs smoothly and efficiently, with all duties and taxes properly calculated and paid. We handle all necessary paperwork and coordinate with local authorities on your behalf.

We provide multiple tracking options for your convenience:

  • Online tracking through our customer portal with real-time updates
  • Automated email notifications at key milestones
  • Mobile app with push notifications
  • Direct access to your dedicated account manager

You'll receive tracking information as soon as your shipment is booked.
