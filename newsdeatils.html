Samman Trans Expands Operations to Southeast Asia

June 15, 2023 By Admin 1,245 Views

Mohammad Al-Samman

CEO, Samman Trans

Share:

Samman Trans is proud to announce the expansion of our operations to key markets in Southeast Asia, including Vietnam, Thailand, and Malaysia. This strategic move allows us to better serve our clients with direct routes and faster transit times in this rapidly growing region.

Port of Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam - One of our new operational hubs

Strategic Expansion into Emerging Markets

After months of careful planning and market research, Samman Trans has officially launched operations in three key Southeast Asian markets:

  • Vietnam: With our new office in Ho Chi Minh City, we can now offer comprehensive logistics solutions for one of Asia's fastest-growing economies.
  • Thailand: Our Bangkok hub provides strategic access to both manufacturing centers and regional distribution networks.
  • Malaysia: The Port Klang facility serves as our gateway to the ASEAN region, offering excellent connectivity.
"This expansion represents a significant milestone in our company's growth strategy. Southeast Asia's dynamic economies present tremendous opportunities for our clients, and we're excited to bring our expertise and reliable services to this important region."
— Mohammad Al-Samman, CEO

Enhanced Service Offerings

With this expansion, Samman Trans clients will benefit from:

Direct Ocean Freight Routes

Weekly sailings between Middle East ports and Southeast Asia with competitive transit times.

Air Freight Solutions

Priority access to cargo space with major carriers serving the region.

Regional Distribution

Strategic warehousing locations for efficient inventory management.

Customs Expertise

Local knowledge to navigate complex import/export regulations.

Why Southeast Asia?

The decision to expand into Southeast Asia was driven by several factors:

  1. Economic Growth: The region has consistently outpaced global GDP growth rates, with manufacturing and trade volumes increasing rapidly.
  2. Client Demand: Many of our existing clients have been expanding their operations in these markets and requested our support.
  3. Strategic Location: Southeast Asia serves as both a production hub and a growing consumer market, creating two-way trade opportunities.
  4. Trade Agreements: The ASEAN free trade area and various bilateral agreements make this region particularly attractive for logistics providers.

One of our vessels loading cargo at a Southeast Asian port

Commitment to Quality Service

While expanding geographically, we remain committed to the same high standards of service that have made Samman Trans a trusted name in logistics:

  • 24/7 customer support with local language capabilities
  • Real-time tracking for all shipments
  • Dedicated account managers for business clients
  • Stringent quality control measures
  • Competitive pricing with no hidden fees

Future Plans

This initial expansion is just the beginning of our Southeast Asia strategy. Over the next 18 months, we plan to:

  • Open additional offices in Indonesia and the Philippines
  • Establish our own bonded warehouses in key locations
  • Develop specialized services for industries like electronics, automotive, and perishables
  • Expand our local workforce with trained logistics professionals

We're excited about this new chapter in Samman Trans' growth and look forward to serving our clients with enhanced capabilities across Southeast Asia.
