Comprehensive Road Freight Services

Samman Trans provides dependable road transport solutions with our modern fleet of trucks and trailers. We offer scheduled and on-demand services for full truckload (FTL) and less than truckload (LTL) shipments throughout Syria and across borders.

Our experienced drivers and logistics team ensure your cargo arrives safely and on time, with real-time tracking available for all shipments. We handle all types of goods, from general merchandise to temperature-controlled and hazardous materials.

Our Road Transport Solutions

Tailored road freight services to meet your specific logistics requirements

Full Truckload (FTL)

Exclusive use of an entire truck for your shipment, ideal for large volumes or sensitive cargo requiring dedicated transport.

  • Dedicated vehicle for your cargo only
  • Faster transit with no intermediate stops
  • Better security and reduced handling
  • Various trailer types available
  • Customized loading/unloading
Request FTL Quote

Less Than Truckload (LTL)

Cost-effective solution for smaller shipments that don't require a full truck, with consolidated loads to optimize efficiency.

  • Pay only for space you use
  • Regular scheduled departures
  • Advanced cargo tracking
  • Palletized and loose cargo options
  • Regional distribution network
Request LTL Quote

Road Transport FAQs

Answers to common questions about our road freight services

Our diverse fleet includes:

  • Standard box trucks (3.5T to 24T capacity)
  • Flatbed and curtain-sided trailers
  • Refrigerated trucks and trailers
  • Low-bed trailers for heavy equipment
  • Tankers for liquid cargo
  • Specialized vehicles for dangerous goods

We can recommend the optimal vehicle type based on your cargo specifications.

We implement multiple safety measures:

  • GPS tracking on all vehicles
  • Experienced, vetted drivers
  • Proper cargo securing and load distribution
  • Secure parking facilities
  • 24/7 monitoring center
  • Comprehensive insurance options

For high-value cargo, we can provide additional security measures upon request.

Standard transit times between major cities:

  • Damascus to Aleppo: 1 day
  • Damascus to Homs: 4-6 hours
  • Damascus to Latakia: 6-8 hours
  • Damascus to Daraa: 2-3 hours
  • Aleppo to Qamishli: 1-2 days

Expedited services are available for urgent shipments. Cross-border transit times vary by destination and border crossing procedures.
