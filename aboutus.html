Connecting Your World Since 1999

Having maintained its leading position in the logistics sector with its 25 years of experience, SAMMAN TRANS offers wide range of services in the freight sector over the local and international market.

Since its foundation in 1999, and through direct control of the entire supply chain, SAMMAN TRANS adds value to its clients' logistics. We develop innovative and alternative solutions in the logistics sector with its end-to-end service principle and multimodal transportation competence.

Our Journey

Key milestones in our history

1999 - Damascus Tower Office

Official opening of our first office in Damascus Tower. Became a member of FIATA.

2000 - First Collouding Agency

Partnership with TUSCOR Lloyds UK as first collouding agency.

2005 - Adraa Tal Kurdi Facilities

Expanded offices and compound in Adraa Tal Kurdi and built our warehouses.

2009 - Airport Free Zone

Established offices and warehouse in the Airport Free Zone.

2012 - Added New Truck Fleet

Expanded logistics capabilities with a new fleet of trucks.

2023 - Bonded Warehouse

Established bonded warehouse in Adraa Free Zone.

2024 - Conqueror Network

Joined Conqueror Freight Network.
